Delegation Guidelines
Important Notes:
  • Start date cannot be in the past
  • End date must be after start date
  • You can only delegate to users with Approver or Admin role
  • You cannot delegate to yourself
  • Overlapping delegations will be prevented
  • You can cancel active delegations at any time
  • Delegations automatically expire at the end date
Use Cases:
  • Vacation or time off
  • Business trips
  • Temporary role changes
  • Workload distribution
